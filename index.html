About

Hi! My name is Hilrick Louw and I am a self taught frontend web developer. I decided to finally take the leap into the amazing, wonderous, magical world of web development in 2020. It has been an incredibily tough, but rewarding journey for me thus far.
I had a successful career as a self taught video editor and director, but my passion has always been tech, games, inovation and the FUTURE!
I am a husband and father of two children a sassy little girl and an incredibly active boy.I did the basic A+ computer course in 2001 and the plan was to go into game development, but things changed as the finances were not available for me to continue that path back then.
After a few years in the hospitality and music industry I started shooting music videos and had to learn video editing. I got an oppertunity to work as an editor on a mid day South African tv show which led to me eventually directing and entire season.
Thoughout this time I have always been keeping a close eye on technology trends and games. In 2020 just before covide my wife and I planned on coming to New Zealand, but then covid happened, She returned on her own as she is a citizen. I made the decision to start studying web development in my own time, which was tough.
I have been getting some help and mentorship from a good friend of mine Willem, who is a senior software engineer currently living in Amsterdam. I have at least 2 other friends who have also been willing to help and guide me through this journey. Willem has been giving me some tasks and work to do on some of the projects he is working on.
Just about three years later and I believe I am ready. I am motivated, I pride myself in my ability to find answers to questions. I am incredibily excited to finally be able to embark on the next step in my life.
